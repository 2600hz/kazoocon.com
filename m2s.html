In this session, Alex will discuss strategies for the start of the sale, coordinating the service and roll out and through post sale experience with VoIP, including topics such as:
  • Understanding subscription based selling with VoIP.
  • Hurdles from selling or clients with traditional PBX's to VoIP.
  • PBX vs. VoIP features and solutions, and setting client expectations.
  • Proper deployment, training and client experience for smooth transitions.
  • Added best practices , such as where to begin with API's and KAZOO, taxation and billing software, training solutions for clients, and more.
