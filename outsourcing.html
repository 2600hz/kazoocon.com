With the landscape constantly changing in UC, the desire for CPaaS functionality is growing at a rapid pace. Imagine if you could create your own app/functionality without having being dependent on a development team or engineers. Well a dream like that can come true. In this session you'll learn about valuable outsourcing resources you can utilize, what skill sets you should look for, basic requirements you'll need to identify and how you can make the dream of creating your own app a reality.
