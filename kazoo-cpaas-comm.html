Imagine if a device could simplify conferencing and how we use it in today's business world. Grouper.ai is a device that makes voice enabled conferencing simple. Grouper focuses on providing simple voice commands to remove the complexity from setting up conference calls, and let groups focus on being groups wherever they are. The Kazoo CPaaS has allowed us to focus on the hard problems of our system, while bringing a vast array of features seamlessly via their vast system of integration APIs and real time streams.
