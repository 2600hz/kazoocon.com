The Commmunications Revolution

October 17th & 18th, 2016 / San Francisco, CA

Register

KazooCon brings together developers, managed service providers, carriers and telecom evangelists to help shape the future of Unified Communications. For Kazoocon 2016, the focus is on how Kazoo enables Service Providers to deliver unique value to end users and drive more recurring revenue. Demos and talks will focus on fully functional applications that exist to save time and reduce cost, and are ready to deploy today. Join us for the Communications Revolution!
