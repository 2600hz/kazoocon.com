Disrupting the Future of Telecom

San Diego: April 30th - May 2nd

Join us for the 5th annual KAZOOcon conference! Each year, KAZOOcon brings together developers, managed service and internet providers, carriers, and telecom evangelists to shape the future of communications, discuss evolving technologies and share best practices to help better the telecom community.

Welcome to KAZOOcon

As a KAZOOcon attendee, you’ll get to hear cutting edge ideas from telecom pioneers, network with thought leaders and technical wizards, and be part of bleeding-edge telecom and mobile technology development. Enjoy informational business and engineering workshops, an unforgettable afterparty and speakers.

We began KAZOOcon with our users in mind, so by joining, you’ll get early access to 2600Hz’s annual roadmap, as well as an opportunity to provide your feedback on major industry trends and how we can better align KAZOO to these advancements.

This year, we’ve added a hackathon to our agenda! Battle it out with other users, creators and integrators of our forever growing product and see what you can create together!

Previously, KAZOOcon SOLD OUT weeks before the event, so early registration is encouraged. Join us on our mission to evolve, simplify and open the cryptic black box of telecom at KAZOOcon 2018. To learn more about 2600Hz’s flagship product KAZOO, click here
