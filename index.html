THE
COMMUNICATIONS
REVOLUTION

Inspired by Kazoo’s open-source platform and hosted by 2600Hz, KazooCon brings together developers, managed service providers, carriers and telecom evangelists to help shape the future of communications. 2015 has been a busy year for us at 2600Hz, we have been hard at work in creating some game-changing telco products. KazooCon is where all these amazing products will be unwrapped including our new reseller platform, WebRTC, Cluster Manager and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). Whoops we’ve already said too much! You will get to hear about the great progress our partners have made, network with a dynamic group of attendees and take part in incredible hands-on experiences. Join us for the Communications Revolution!
