Why Attend

About KAZOOcon

KAZOOcon is inspired by KAZOO - 2600Hz’s advanced carrier grade UCaaS/CPaaS solution. 2600Hz’s revolutionary platform lets you do business your way, ultimately empowering resellers to customize every part of their UC solution, including pricing, branding, customer offerings and more.



What You’ll Gain

Whether you’re looking for the most recent advancements across UCaaS or CPaaS technologies, cutting edge best practices, or valuable networking opportunities, KAZOOcon 2019 will deliver an experience to remember.

As a KAZOOcon attendee, you will hear about the latest UC products and VoIP enhancements from telecom pioneers, mingle with industry experts and be part of bleeding-edge telecom and mobile technology developments. You’ll get early access to 2600Hz roadmap updates, as well as a chance to share your input on where the industry is going and what you want to see from KAZOO.



An Interactive Experience

Your time is important to us! So is ensuring each and every attendee finds value through insightful educational sessions and unparalleled networking engagements. From an interactive and innovative Hackathon to kickoff the adventure, to welcome receptions, after-parties and roundtable lunch sessions, this event will have it all.

Location

After last year’s success, KAZOOcon is back in San Diego at the Bahia Resort Hotel. Located on a 14-acre peninsula on the west shore of Mission Bay, the resort is just 8 miles from downtown San Diego and less than a 15 minute drive from San Diego International Airport.

The Bahia Resort Hotel is San Diego’s premier beachfront destination boasting lush tropical gardens and miles of sandy beaches. FAQs

General


What is KAZOOcon?

KAZOOcon, inspired by 2600Hz’s advanced carrier grade UCaaS solution KAZOO, is an annual gathering of industry leaders, technical masters, creative developers and telecom evangelists to explore the evolving technologies and cutting edge best practices across unified communications.



Who attends KAZOOcon?

Last year, many C-level business leaders and decision makers across a variety of market segments, including MSPs, ISPs, carriers, VARs, integrators, and service providers attended KAZOOcon.



When is KAZOOcon?

Tuesday, June 18, 2019 - Thursday, June 20, 2019



Where is KAZOOcon?

The Bahia Resort Hotel, located at 998 West Mission Bay Drive in San Diego, CA.



Registration & Lodging


How do I register?

  • Welcome Reception (Tuesday, June 18th)

How early should I register?

The earlier the better! It really is up to you, earlier tickets have a lower price. So if you want to get the best deal, make sure to purchase the early bird tickets and book a room at the Bahia. But we will always make room for one more last minute attendee.



What is the KAZOOcon cancellation/substitution policy?

Attendees can receive refunds up to 7 days before the event start date (June 18th, 2019).



Are there specific hotel rates?

We have secured the best discounted rate for you! Each room is $199 per night and the daily resort fee is waived. Just use code "2600Hz" or reserve your room here. Discounted rooms are limited, so be sure to reserve your room as early as possible.



What is the hotel cancellation policy?

The hotel cancellation policy is handled directly through the Bahia Resort Hotel. Your specific cancellation policy will be displayed or quoted at the time of reservation, and also appear in your reservation confirmation email. Reservations cancelled after your cancellation deadline will be subject to a cancellation fee of one night room and tax.



Travel


How do I get from the airport to the Bahia Resort Hotel?

Taxis and ride sharing services such as Lyft and Uber are the quickest and most convenient methods of transportation from the San Diego Airport to the Bahia Resort Hotel. An Uber/Lyft will cost approximately $10 - $20 but can vary upon demand. If you plan on renting a car, there is free on-site parking included with your room reservation at the resort (one car per room).



What is the weather like in San Diego in June?

It’s gorgeous! The average high is 71℉ with little to no precipitation. The average low is 62℉, so you may want to pack a light jacket or sweater for the evenings. Take a look at the average temperatures in San Diego here.



Where can I find information about San Diego and local attractions?

The Bahia Resort Hotel is conveniently located close to downtown San Diego as well as many of the area’s popular attractions. For information about local attractions, check out The Bahia Resort Hotel’s neighborhood Guide here.
