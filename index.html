Join us for the 5th annual KAZOOcon conference! Get Informed

As a KAZOOcon attendee, you’ll get to hear cutting edge ideas from telecom pioneers, network with thought leaders and technical wizards, and be part of bleeding-edge telecom and mobile technology development. Yealink is a global leading unified communication (UC) solution provider that primarily offers video conferencing systems and voice communication solutions. Founded in 2001, Yealink leverages its independent research and development and innovation to pursue its core mission: “Easy collaboration, high productivity.” The company’s high-quality UC solutions enhance the work efficiency and competitive advantages of its customers in over 100 countries. Yealink is the world’s second-largest SIP phone provider and is number one in the China market

R Squared Telecom is a carrier specializing in high-fidelity SIP trunking and numbering resources. With our API-driven products, you can swiftly deliver services to your customers. Our approach is customized to each client: designed to enhance your systems while managing your costs. R Squared enables you to operate, communicate, and grow exponentially. Get started today at RSquaredTelecom.com!

Gold Sponsor

Rev.io helps Communications and IoT service providers optimize their metered and recurring billing, build positive relationships with customers, avoid revenue leakage and grow their business. Our sophisticated billing-as-a-service (Baas) platform is an all-in-one solution for managing end customers' subscriptions, usage, taxation, billing and payments. Visit our website to learn more.

Telnyx delivers voice, messaging and more for applications and next-generation communications companies. We're a communications platform that provides global carrier-grade services. Maintaining an international, private IP network. we grant our customers unprecedented control over their communications through our innovative cloud portal and RESTful API.

Telnyx products include voice (elastic SIP trunking, global number search, and telephony data), programmatic messaging, embedded communications (WebRTC) and automated networking. Customers provision services a la carte and pay by usage for scalable, on-demand communications.

Silver Sponsor

Established in 2006, ChikPea provides Salesforce-native solutions that consolidate telecom Subscriber Relationship Management functions and accelerates lead to revenue process and visibility for everyone. We are the first subscriber retention specialty firm on the Salesforce platform that builds the flexible software tools to allow telecom companies to use all aspects of Variable-Price-Modeling (VPM)© and order management to win and gain market share.

VoIP Innovations (VI) is a provider of carrier services in the US, Canada and with SIP coverage for origination in over 60 countries. They recently became a CLEC which enables them to provide their own numbers in addition to aggregating a network of many nationwide carriers at aggressive wholesale prices.They’ve been in business since 2007 and have earned the trust of thousands of customers.

VI expanded with new CPaaS programmable voice and SMS capabilities through their acquisition of Apidaze. Apidaze offers state-of-the art communication APIs and a Showroom of ready-to-market communication applications that can be deployed right off the Showroom floor.

Since 2006, 888VoIP has been a leading distributor of VoIP hardware and software solutions. 888VoIP is committed to driving the customers’ success and does so by identifying and understanding customers’ specific requirements and unique needs. By providing customers with the most innovative products, full cycle logistics, wholesale discounts, dedicated staff, and access to the Channel Advantage program, 888VoIP can assure the success of partners’ projects.

Founded in 2014, Innovista Law is an international telecommunications and technology law practice that’s designed using the latest technology and smart work environments to foster inventive thinking and provide superior client service at a lower cost. Innovista’s attorneys have decades of combined experience representing businesses on their thorniest telecommunications and technology issues.
