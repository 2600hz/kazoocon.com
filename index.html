A Communications Revolution

presented by 2600hz October 14-15, 2013 in San Francisco, California
  • Register
  • Until the Revolution begins!

2600hz is proud to present KazooCon, our inaugural conference that provides an unbeatable concoction of pioneers in telecom, astounding demos, and the best educational atmosphere in the business. Stop the clock and engage in hands-on experiences focused around distributed communication networks. It’s an event packed with knowledge, fun, and networking…who wouldn’t want to be a part of that?!

Who Should Attend

  • Communications Architects
  • Entrepreneurs and Thought Leaders
  • Passionate Developers

On The Agenda

  • Overview of WebRTC, TelcoSDN, and Distributed Tech
  • The history of telecom—from Signal Fires to LTE
  • Engage with 2600hz Customers and experience hands-on demos

Guaranteed Fun

  • Endless hours of nerdy conversations
  • Lots of coffee, gourmet food, and art performances
  • A fun and engaging afterparty—be there or else
